ABOUT US

Solar Edge established in 1998, with the headquarter in Lekki Lagos, specialises in the design, supply, installation and maintenance of all types of CCTV systems. We also specialise in the closely related field of Access Control and Video Entry Systems.

Free site surveys are conducted professionally and confidentially, where an experienced surveyor will listen to all requirements concentrating on any particular issues or areas of concern that there may be who will design a system to best fit the remit without any obligation.
In just a few years, SOLAR EDGE have become synonymous with mastery in CCTV Products and installation, technological innovation, build quality and unparalleled customer service and are highly spoken of by our customers worldwide.
